## Overlap ablation (stop rule; paired vs. the same config at zero overlap)

`fixed` overlap is in tokens, `sentence` overlap in sentences. The budget charges every retrieved chunk its full token count (duplicated text included) while scoring uses the union of retrieved tokens, so overlap must earn back its redundancy to break even.
